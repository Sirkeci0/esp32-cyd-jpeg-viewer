JPEG Viewer for ESP32-2432S028
Views files from SD card. Put your jpg or jpeg files to /jpeg folder in SD card.
Auto rotation feature.
Touchscreen divided to 4 areas.
Press top left to go back 10 files. Press top right to go forward 10 files.
Press bottom left to go back 1 file. Press bottom right to go forward 1 file.
Named "Sirkeci Frankenstein JPEG Viewer" because I merged two diffrent codes.
